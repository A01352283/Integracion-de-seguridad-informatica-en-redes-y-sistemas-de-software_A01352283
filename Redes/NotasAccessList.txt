Listas de acceso

Access-list <numero> (permit|deny) (any|host <ip> | <ip> <wildcard>)

Ejemplos:
access-list 1 deny host 10.0.0.15
access-list 1 permit any
access-list 1 permit 10.0.30.0 0.0.0.255
